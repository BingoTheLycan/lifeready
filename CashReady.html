❓ HOW TO PLAY
🏪 WHAT IS CASHREADY?
★
CashReady helps you practice counting money, making change, and real cashier skills - at your own pace, your own way. Free for everyone, no account needed.
🎮 ALL THE MODES
👀
Watch a Demo - See exactly how to make change step by step before you try. Great for first-timers.
⭐
Starter Mode - Coins only. Customer always pays $1. No pressure. Perfect place to begin.
🎓
Practice Mode - No score, no pressure. Unlimited hints. Take as long as you need.
▶
Start Shift - Full cashier simulation. Score, streaks, levels. 10 customers per shift.
☀️
Daily Challenge - 5 quick transactions every day. Build a streak and earn milestone badges at 3, 7, 14, 30, and 60 days!
🧠 LEARNING TOOLS
🛒
Do I Have Enough? - A wallet full of coins, an item with a price tag. Can you afford it? Practice the real-life skill of knowing before you get to the register.
💵
Money ID Quiz - Identify coins AND bills, learn portraits, test your knowledge. 10 random questions each time from a pool of 35.
📚
Money Guide - A pressure-free reference for all coins and bills. Tap any coin or bill to hear it read aloud. Three tabs: Coins, Bills, and Combos.
📋 HOW TO MAKE CHANGE
1
Look at CHANGE DUE on the receipt - that is exactly how much to give back.
2
Tap coins and bills from the REGISTER DRAWER to add them to the change tray.
3
Watch the CHANGE TRAY bar fill up. Stop when it hits FULL - not over!
4
Tap DISPENSE CHANGE. Made a mistake? Tap UNDO to remove the last item, or tap any chip in the tray to remove it.
🔢 COUNT-UP METHOD ⭐
★
Real cashiers do not subtract - they count up from the price to what was paid.

Item: $1.25 · Paid: $2.00
"$1.50 ... $1.75 ... $2.00 - 75¢!"

Enable Count-Up Guide in Settings to see this method while you play.

💚 THE STORY

CashReady is the first app in the LifeReady suite - and it was created by someone who knows what it is like to need this app.

I am an adult with a developmental disability. I have sat in the chair that a lot of you have sat in - working on life skills, building independence, figuring out how the world works in ways that do not always come easy. I know what it feels like when a tool is not made with you in mind.

So I built the app I wished existed.

CashReady started as a way to help people like me practice money skills - counting coins, making change, getting ready for a real job at a real register. But I wanted it to be more than that. I wanted it to be something that works for everybody - young or old, with a disability or without, whether you are just learning or just need a refresher.

🚀 THE MISSION

Too many apps built for people with disabilities feel like they were made about them, not by or for them. They are clinical. They are boring. They do not feel like something you'd actually want to use.

CashReady is different because it comes from the inside. Every accessibility feature, every learning mode, every piece of feedback was designed with real people in mind - people who deserve tools that respect their intelligence, celebrate their progress, and meet them exactly where they are.

This app is for the person in a day program working toward their first job. It is for the special ed student who learns differently. It is for the job coach sitting beside a learner who needs the right tool. And it is for anyone at any age who just wants to get better with money - no judgment, no pressure, just practice.

🤝 A DEDICATION

This app is dedicated to every job coach, special education teacher, and vocational instructor who shows up every day for people who need them. Your work changes lives. CashReady is built to make your work a little easier.

And it is dedicated to every person out there who has ever been told what they cannot do - and decided to prove otherwise.

💵 PART OF THE LIFEREADY SUITE

CashReady is part of the LifeReady suite - a growing set of free, accessible life skills trainers built by the same person, for the same purpose.
