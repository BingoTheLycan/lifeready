🏠 WHAT IS HOMEREADY?
★
HomeReady helps you practice real home and life skills - laundry, budgeting, food safety, emergencies, and renting. Free for everyone, no account needed.

💰

🚨

🍳

📋

🏷️ LAUNDRY GUIDE
🫧 Wash
🌀 Dry
🔥 Iron
🎨 Sort
WASHING SYMBOLS
❄️
Cold Wash (30°C) - For colors, delicates. Prevents shrinking and bleeding.
💧
Warm Wash (40°C) - For cottons and most everyday clothes.
♨️
Hot Wash (60°C) - For whites, towels, bedding. Kills germs.
✋
Hand Wash Only - Too delicate for any machine. Use the sink.
🚫
Do Not Wash - Dry clean only. Take to a professional.

💡 When in doubt, use cold. Cold water rarely damages clothes and works for most everyday items.

DRYING SYMBOLS
🌀
Tumble Dry - Normal dryer heat. Good for cottons.
🌡️
Low Heat - For stretchy or thin fabrics. Prevents shrinkage.
👕
Hang Dry - Air dry on a hanger or clothesline.
📐
Lay Flat - Spread on a flat surface. Important for wool and knits.
🚫
No Dryer - Heat will damage this fabric. Air dry only.
IRONING GUIDE
🔥
High Heat - Cotton and linen. Cotton setting on the iron.
🌡️
Medium Heat - Polyester and wool blends.
❄️
Low Heat - Silk and delicates.
💨
Steam OK - You can use the steam function safely.
🚫
Do Not Iron - Heat will damage this fabric.
SORTING GUIDE
⬜
Whites alone - Always separate. Even faint colors can make whites grey.
🔴
New darks separate - New reds and darks bleed. Wash with similar colors first.
💙
Colors together - Medium and dark colors usually OK together on cold.
🧤
Delicates separate - Bras, lace, silk - mesh bag on gentle or hand wash.
👖
Heavy separate - Jeans and towels together is fine. Not with delicates.
🧾 BILLS GUIDE
READING A UTILITY BILL
SAMPLE ELECTRIC BILL
Account Name: Your Name
Account Number: 123-456-789
Service Period: Jan 1 - Jan 31
Due Date: February 15
Amount Due: $67.42
📅
Due Date - Pay before this date to avoid late fees. Set a phone reminder.
💰
Amount Due - How much you owe this month. Changes based on usage.
📆
Service Period - The dates this bill covers. Usually about 30 days.
#
Past Due - You missed a previous payment. Pay immediately to avoid disconnection.

⚠️ Cannot pay? Call BEFORE the due date. Most utility companies have payment plans or extensions if you ask early.

COMMON BILLS
💡
Electric - Powers lights, outlets, appliances.
💧
Water - Drinking, bathing, toilets. Sometimes included in rent.
🔥
Gas - Stove or heating. Not all homes have this.
🌐
Internet - Your WiFi. Usually a fixed monthly amount.
🏠
Rent - Due same day every month. Always pay on time.
📋 RENTING GUIDE
KEY TERMS
📄
Lease - A written contract between you and your landlord. Always read before signing.
💰
Security Deposit - Usually 1-2 months rent paid upfront. You get it back when you move out if there's no damage.
👤
Landlord - The person or company that owns the property.
🏠
Tenant - You. The person renting and living there.
📬
Notice - When moving out, tell your landlord at least 30 days in advance.

💡 If something breaks, tell your landlord in writing right away - text or email - so there is a record.

YOUR RIGHTS AS A TENANT
✅
Landlords must give 24-48 hours notice before entering your home, except in emergencies.
✅
You have the right to a habitable home - working heat, no major leaks, no infestations.
✅
Your security deposit must be returned within 14-30 days after you move out.
✅
You cannot be evicted without proper legal notice - usually 30 days written notice.
🍳 FOOD SAFETY
FRIDGE VS PANTRY
❄️ KEEP IN FRIDGE
Milk & dairy
Eggs
Raw meat
Leftovers
Cut fruit/veg
Opened juice
Butter & mayo
🍞 KEEP IN PANTRY
Bread & pasta
Canned goods
Rice & beans
Cereal
Cooking oils
Honey
Whole fruits
EXPIRATION DATES
⚠️
Use By / Expires - Do not eat after this date. May not be safe.
📅
Best By / Best Before - Best quality before this date. May still be okay shortly after.
🏪
Sell By - This is for the store, not you. Usually still good a few days after.

⚠️ Never leave cooked food out more than 2 hours. Bacteria grow fast at room temperature. When in doubt, throw it out.

🧹 CLEANING GUIDE
CLEANING ROUTINE
📅
Daily (5-10 min) - Wash dishes, wipe kitchen counters, take out full trash bags.
📆
Weekly (30-60 min) - Vacuum or sweep, clean bathroom sink and toilet, do laundry, wipe all surfaces.
🗓️
Monthly - Clean inside the fridge, wipe the stovetop, clean the shower or tub, dust shelves and furniture.
⚠️ NEVER MIX THESE
🚫
Bleach + Ammonia - Creates toxic fumes. Dangerous and potentially deadly.
🚫
Bleach + Vinegar - Creates chlorine gas. Very dangerous.
🚫
Bleach + Rubbing Alcohol - Creates harmful fumes.
✅
Use one cleaner at a time. Rinse surfaces well before switching products.
📞 CALL SCRIPTS
CALLING YOUR LANDLORD

"Hi, my name is [your name] and I live at [your address], apartment [number]. I am calling to report that [describe the problem]. Can someone come take a look? My number is [your number]. Thank you."

CALLING A UTILITY COMPANY

"Hi, I am calling about my account. My account number is [number]. I have a question about my bill for [month] - specifically the charge for [amount]. Can you help me understand that?"

CALLING 911

Only call 911 for real emergencies - fire, medical emergency, or crime in progress.

"I need help at [your full address]. [Describe what is happening.]"

Stay on the line. They will tell you what to do. Do not hang up.

💡 Not everything needs 911. For noise complaints, minor fender benders, or small local issues - search "[your city] non-emergency police number."

🧡 THE STORY

HomeReady was built because home skills are the foundation of everything else.

I built CashReady to teach money skills. I built TimeReady to teach clock and schedule skills. I built JobReady to teach workplace readiness. But the whole time, I kept thinking about something more fundamental - what about the skills you need before any of that? The skills you need just to keep a roof over your head and food in your fridge?

How do you sort laundry? How do you read a utility bill? What do you do when you smell gas? What does your lease actually say? These are not complicated things - but if nobody taught you, they can feel impossible.

HomeReady is the fourth piece of the LifeReady puzzle - and in a lot of ways, the most important one. Home is where everything starts.

🚀 THE MISSION

Independent living should not be a mystery. For a lot of people - especially people with disabilities, people aging out of foster care, people transitioning from supported living - the gap between knowing something and being able to practice it safely is huge.

HomeReady closes that gap. It gives you a safe place to practice real home skills - laundry, budgeting, food safety, emergencies, renting - at your own pace, with no pressure and no cost.

Every question is written for real life. Every hint is real guidance. Every badge celebrates real progress. This app is for the person working toward their first apartment. For the life skills instructor helping a client build independence. For anyone who just needs the right tool.

🤝 A DEDICATION

This app is dedicated to every support worker, life skills instructor, and direct care professional who shows up - often without recognition, always with heart - to help people build the lives they deserve.

And it is dedicated to everyone who has ever had to figure out adult life without a roadmap. You are not behind. You are exactly where you need to be - and HomeReady is here to help you get where you want to go.
