Mission Control Guide

How Mission Control Works

One learner. One dashboard.

Mission Control reads progress from every LifeReady module and keeps it in one educator-friendly view. No more module hopping just to explain how one person is doing.

Log what matters.

Add session notes, staff initials, setting, score, and support level. That turns game activity into usable evidence instead of a pile of disconnected numbers.

Use objectives and reports.

Track progress toward practical goals, watch rank progression, and export summaries that are clean enough for meetings, supervisors, and state review.

Learner data stays on this device unless you choose to export a report or CSV. The sci-fi glow is for morale. The professionalism is still very much on purpose.
