🕐 CLOCK GUIDE
🕐 HOW A CLOCK WORKS
1
A clock has two hands. The short hand points to the hour. The long hand points to the minutes.
2
The numbers 1-12 go around the clock. The short hand takes 1 hour to move from one number to the next.
3
The long hand takes 5 minutes to move between each number. When it points to 12, it means 0 minutes - exactly on the hour.
4
When the long hand points to 6, it means 30 minutes - we call this "half past" the hour.
⏰ READING THE CLOCK

Here are some common times to practice recognizing:

🌅 AM AND PM
☀️
AM means the morning - from midnight (12:00 AM) to noon (12:00 PM). Work shifts that start in the morning use AM times like 9:00 AM.
🌙
PM means the afternoon and evening - from noon to midnight. Afternoon shifts use PM times like 2:00 PM or 5:30 PM.
📋 JOB SCHEDULE TIPS
💡
Always check if your shift time is AM or PM. A 9:00 shift could be morning or evening - the AM/PM tells you which.
💡
To find out how long until your shift, count the hours from the current time to your start time. If it is 11:00 AM and you start at 2:00 PM, that is 3 hours.
💡
A typical work shift is 4-8 hours. If you start at 9:00 AM and work 4 hours, you finish at 1:00 PM.

💙 THE STORY

TimeReady is the second app in the LifeReady suite - created by someone who knows what it is like to need this app.

I am an adult with a developmental disability. After building CashReady - a money skills trainer - I realized the people I was building it for also needed help with another foundational skill: telling time.

So I built the app that should have existed alongside it.

TimeReady started because my pre-vocational class was learning job schedules and nobody had a tool built specifically for that. Reading a clock, understanding AM and PM, knowing how long until your shift starts - these are real independence skills. This app is built to teach them.

🚀 THE MISSION

Too many apps built for people with disabilities feel like they were made about them, not by or for them. They are clinical. They are boring. They do not feel like something you'd actually want to use.

TimeReady is different because it comes from the inside. Every mode, every accessibility feature, every question type was designed with real learners in mind - people who deserve tools that respect their intelligence, celebrate their progress, and meet them exactly where they are.

This app is for the person in a day program who's working toward independence. It is for the job coach helping a client prepare for their first shift. It is for the special ed teacher who needs the right tool in the classroom. And it is for anyone who just wants to get better at telling time - no judgment, no pressure, just practice.

🤝 A DEDICATION

This app is dedicated to every job coach, special education teacher, and vocational instructor who shows up every day for people who need them. Your work changes lives. TimeReady is built to make your work a little easier.

And it is dedicated to every person out there who has ever been told what they cannot do - and decided to prove otherwise.

🆓 ALWAYS FREE
🆓

TimeReady is completely free. No ads, no subscriptions, no paywalls. It was built to help people - and that does not have a price tag.
