рҹ•җ CLOCK GUIDE
рҹ•җ HOW A CLOCK WORKS
1
A clock has two hands. The short hand points to the hour. The long hand points to the minutes.
2
The numbers 1вҖ“12 go around the clock. The short hand takes 1 hour to move from one number to the next.
3
The long hand takes 5 minutes to move between each number. When it points to 12, it means 0 minutes вҖ” exactly on the hour.
4
When the long hand points to 6, it means 30 minutes вҖ” we call this "half past" the hour.
вҸ° READING THE CLOCK

Here are some common times to practice recognizing:

рҹҢ… AM AND PM
вҳҖпёҸ
AM means the morning вҖ” from midnight (12:00 AM) to noon (12:00 PM). Work shifts that start in the morning use AM times like 9:00 AM.
рҹҢҷ
PM means the afternoon and evening вҖ” from noon to midnight. Afternoon shifts use PM times like 2:00 PM or 5:30 PM.
рҹ“Ӣ JOB SCHEDULE TIPS
рҹ’Ў
Always check if your shift time is AM or PM. A 9:00 shift could be morning or evening вҖ” the AM/PM tells you which.
рҹ’Ў
To find out how long until your shift, count the hours from the current time to your start time. If it's 11:00 AM and you start at 2:00 PM, that's 3 hours.
рҹ’Ў
A typical work shift is 4вҖ“8 hours. If you start at 9:00 AM and work 4 hours, you finish at 1:00 PM.

рҹ’ҷ THE STORY

TimeReady is the second app in the LifeReady suite вҖ” created by someone who knows what it's like to need this app.

I'm an adult with a developmental disability. After building CashReady вҖ” a money skills trainer вҖ” I realized the people I was building it for also needed help with another foundational skill: telling time.

So I built the app that should have existed alongside it.

TimeReady started because my pre-vocational class was learning job schedules and nobody had a tool built specifically for that. Reading a clock, understanding AM and PM, knowing how long until your shift starts вҖ” these are real independence skills. This app is built to teach them.

рҹҡҖ THE MISSION

Too many apps built for people with disabilities feel like they were made about them, not by or for them. They're clinical. They're boring. They don't feel like something you'd actually want to use.

TimeReady is different because it comes from the inside. Every mode, every accessibility feature, every question type was designed with real learners in mind вҖ” people who deserve tools that respect their intelligence, celebrate their progress, and meet them exactly where they are.

This app is for the person in a day program who's working toward independence. It's for the job coach helping a client prepare for their first shift. It's for the special ed teacher who needs the right tool in the classroom. And it's for anyone who just wants to get better at telling time вҖ” no judgment, no pressure, just practice.

рҹӨқ A DEDICATION

This app is dedicated to every job coach, special education teacher, and vocational instructor who shows up every day for people who need them. Your work changes lives. TimeReady is built to make your work a little easier.

And it's dedicated to every person out there who has ever been told what they can't do вҖ” and decided to prove otherwise.

рҹҶ“ ALWAYS FREE
рҹҶ“

TimeReady is completely free. No ads, no subscriptions, no paywalls. It was built to help people вҖ” and that doesn't have a price tag.
