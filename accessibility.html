Accessibility

LifeReady is built to support a wide range of learners, including people who benefit from repetition, simple layouts, visual structure, audio support, and low-pressure practice.

  • Works on phones, tablets, and computers.
  • Uses large tap targets and clear module cards.
  • Includes repeat practice, visual cues, and supportive feedback.
  • Supports learners, families, teachers, coaches, and day programs.

Accessibility is an ongoing process. If something is confusing, hard to read, hard to hear, or hard to use, that is worth improving.
