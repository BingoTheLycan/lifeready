Accessibility

LifeReady is designed to support a wide range of learners, including people who benefit from repetition, simple layouts, visual structure, audio support, and low-pressure practice.

  • Works on phones, tablets, and computers.
  • Includes large tap targets, clear module cards, and built-in text-size controls such as the Aa button in supported modules.
  • Includes repeat practice, visual cues, audio supports in parts of the app, and supportive feedback.
  • Offers settings in different modules such as larger text, larger buttons, contrast options, reduced motion, and other learner-friendly supports.
  • Supports learners, families, teachers, coaches, and day programs.

Accessibility is an ongoing process, and support can vary a little between modules while the app continues to improve. If something is confusing, hard to read, hard to hear, or hard to use, that is worth improving.

For feedback about accessibility barriers, readability issues, or device-specific problems, please use the contact page so those issues can be reviewed and fixed.
