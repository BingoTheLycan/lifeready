Mission Brief

Life skills practice should feel clear, repeatable, and worth coming back to.

LifeReady is built to reduce pressure without reducing purpose. It supports repetition, straightforward feedback, strong visual structure, and a game-like feel without talking down to people.

✦
For learnersPlay independently or with support, repeat what helps, and build confidence one skill at a time.
✦
For coaches, educators, and support teamsUse the modules as practice tools, discussion starters, transition supports, or warmups in schools, day programs, and community settings.
✦
For real lifeEverything points back to practical independence, not just points on a screen.
Mission Control

Mission Control keeps learner progress in one place.

Track one learner across every module without bouncing between separate screens, notes, or tools.

Review

MANAGE

SHARED DEVICE NOTICE

Using a shared device?

LifeReady can save progress, preferences, and notes on the device being used. On a school, program, family, or work device, other people may be able to see saved activity unless it is cleared later.

Need to reset things later? Clear this site’s browser data on the device you used.
