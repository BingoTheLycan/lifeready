ABOUT LIFEREADY
⭐ WHAT IS LIFEREADY?

LifeReady is a suite of free, accessible life skills trainers - built by someone who knows what it is like to need them.

Every app in the suite focuses on a different set of real-world skills: managing money, reading time, preparing for work, keeping a home, getting around independently, and navigating social situations. Each one is designed to be used by anyone - people with disabilities, people building independence, people in transition, or anyone who just wants to get better at something.

No accounts. No ads. No paywalls. Just practice.

"These tools were built for the person who has been told what they cannot do.
Every skill here says otherwise."
🤝 WHO IT'S FOR

People with developmental disabilities building toward independence. Adults aging out of foster care. People transitioning from supported living. Special education students preparing for adult life. Job coaches and life skills instructors looking for a better tool. Anyone who learns differently and deserves tools that respect that.

If you have ever needed to practice something that most people take for granted - this suite was built for you.
