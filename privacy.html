Privacy

LifeReady is designed to be simple and respectful.

  • No accounts are required.
  • No ads are shown.
  • No personal information is sold.
  • Progress, preferences, and local notes may be stored on the device being used so the app can remember settings and session history.
  • If you share a device, other people using that same device may be able to see locally stored progress unless it is cleared.

If LifeReady is later connected to outside services, this page should be updated before that change is made.
